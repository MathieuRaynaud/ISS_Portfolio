Self evaluation

The Innovative Smart Systems formation allowed me to acquire a lot of skills in the domain of the IoT.

First, I improved importantly my skills in terms of software development. I learned new programming languages like NodeJS, Angular, and Node-Red. I also learned to produce a REST API and web services to interact with an application.

Then, I learned to lead a project using the scrum method.

In terms of hardware development, I obtained a lot of useful information, that allows me to work with hardware components with less difficulties. Some courses, like the Protocols for the connected objects or the Energy for the connected objects, brought me a lot of knowledge that I didn't have at all, and thanks to which I extended my skills importantly.

The formation allowed me to work with colleagues specialized in electronics. This was a great opportunity to teach them all the knowledge I obtained during my previous formation at INSA. It was also a great chance to take benefit of their electronic background, that they brought me with an other approach than the one of the professors during courses.

According to me, the innovative project was a great experience. It allowed me to discover the IoT domain and to apply all the knowledge acquired during my formation at INSA in an innovative project. This was also very enriching to work in a team with several background (electronics engineering, mecatronics engineering and software engineering), and to deal with the knowledge of each member of the team.

Finally, I would say that the ISS formation is a very skillful formation which brought me a lot of competencies in both hardware and software domains. It also allowed me to discover the IoT domain, and to work on an IoT project during four months.
